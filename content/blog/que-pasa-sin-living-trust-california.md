## What Is Probate?

Probate is the legal process where a California court decides how a deceased person's assets are distributed. Even if you have a will, your assets go through this process.

**The only way to avoid probate is to have a Living Trust.**

## What Your Family Faces Without a Living Trust

### 1. Time: 12 to 24 Months of Waiting

The probate process in California takes an average of **12 to 18 months**, and complicated cases can extend to **2 years or more**. During that time:

- Your family can't access bank accounts
- They can't sell the house if they need the money
- Mortgage payments and bills keep coming
- Debts accumulate while they wait

### 2. Costs: Thousands of Dollars in Fees
