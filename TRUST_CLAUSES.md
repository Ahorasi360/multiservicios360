## PHASE 1 - REQUIRED CLAUSES (Always Included)

### 1. DECLARATION OF TRUST
This Declaration of Trust ("Trust") is made by [Trustor Full Legal Name(s)], as Trustor(s), effective as of [Trust Date], for the purpose of creating a trust pursuant to the laws of the State of California.

### 2. IDENTIFICATION OF TRUSTOR(S)
The Trustor(s) of this Trust is/are [Full Legal Name(s)], whose current address is [Address]. Any reference herein to the "Trustor" shall include the Trustor's personal representative or legal successor where applicable.

### 3. TRUST NAME AND DATE
This Trust shall be known as "The [Name] Trust dated [Month Day, Year]", and may be referred to herein as the "Trust."

### 4. INTENT TO CREATE TRUST
The Trustor declares the intention to create a valid and enforceable trust and to transfer property to the Trustee to be held, managed, and distributed in accordance with the terms of this Trust.

### 5. REVOCABILITY (Revocable Version)
During the lifetime of the Trustor, this Trust shall be revocable and amendable, in whole or in part, by the Trustor by a written instrument signed by the Trustor and delivered to the Trustee.

### 5. IRREVOCABILITY (Irrevocable Version)
This Trust is irrevocable and may not be amended, modified, or revoked by the Trustor except as expressly permitted by applicable law.

### 6. GOVERNING LAW (CALIFORNIA)
This Trust shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.

### 7. TRUST PURPOSE
The purpose of this Trust is to provide for the management, conservation, and distribution of the Trust estate for the benefit of the beneficiaries as provided herein.

### 8. TRUST PROPERTY / SCHEDULE A REFERENCE
The Trust estate shall consist of all property transferred to the Trustee by the Trustor, whether described in Schedule A attached hereto or acquired by the Trust thereafter.

### 9. APPOINTMENT OF INITIAL TRUSTEE
The Trustor hereby appoints [Name] as the initial Trustee of this Trust. The Trustee accepts the duties and responsibilities imposed by this Trust.

### 10. SUCCESSOR TRUSTEE DESIGNATION
If the acting Trustee resigns, becomes incapacitated, or otherwise ceases to serve, [Name(s)] shall serve as Successor Trustee(s), in the order listed.

### 11. TRUSTEE ACCEPTANCE
Any Trustee named herein shall evidence acceptance of trusteeship by acting in the capacity of Trustee or by executing a written acceptance.

### 12. GENERAL TRUSTEE POWERS
The Trustee shall have all powers granted under California Probate Code Sections 16200 through 16249, as amended, and any other powers necessary or advisable to carry out the purposes of this Trust, without court authorization.

### 13. POWER TO MANAGE TRUST ASSETS
The Trustee shall have full power to collect, hold, manage, invest, reinvest, sell, exchange, lease, mortgage, insure, and otherwise deal with Trust property as the Trustee deems appropriate.

### 14. POWER TO HIRE PROFESSIONALS
The Trustee may employ attorneys, accountants, financial advisors, and other professionals and may pay reasonable compensation for such services from the Trust estate.

### 15. TRUSTEE COMPENSATION & EXPENSES
The Trustee shall be entitled to reasonable compensation and reimbursement for expenses incurred in the administration of the Trust, unless waived in writing.

### 16. WAIVER OF BOND
No bond shall be required of any Trustee serving under this Trust.

### 17. SPENDTHRIFT PROTECTION (BASELINE)
No interest of any beneficiary shall be subject to voluntary or involuntary transfer, assignment, pledge, or seizure by creditors prior to actual distribution.

### 18. INCAPACITY DEFINITION
The Trustor shall be deemed incapacitated upon written determination by two licensed physicians or one licensed physician and one licensed psychologist, stating that the Trustor is unable to manage financial affairs.

### 19. AUTHORITY UPON INCAPACITY
Upon the Trustor's incapacity, the Successor Trustee shall immediately assume all trustee powers and manage the Trust estate for the Trustor's benefit.

### 20. PAYMENT OF FINAL EXPENSES
Upon the Trustor's death, the Trustee shall pay final medical expenses, funeral costs, and expenses of last illness from the Trust estate.

### 21. PAYMENT OF DEBTS & TAXES
The Trustee shall pay the Trustor's legally enforceable debts and applicable taxes from the Trust estate, subject to applicable law.

### 22. RESIDUAL DISTRIBUTION
After payment of expenses, debts, and taxes, the remaining Trust estate shall be distributed to [Beneficiary/Beneficiaries], in the proportions specified herein.

### 23. SEVERABILITY
If any provision of this Trust is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

### 24. CONSTRUCTION CLAUSE
Words of gender shall include all genders, and words in the singular shall include the plural, where the context so requires.

### 25. HEADINGS NOT CONTROLLING
Headings used in this Trust are for convenience only and shall not affect interpretation.

### 26. EXECUTION – TRUSTOR
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Trustor has executed this Trust as of the date first written above.

### 27. EXECUTION – TRUSTEE
The Trustee acknowledges acceptance of the duties and responsibilities of this Trust.

## PHASE 2 - OPTIONAL CLAUSES (Tier: Plus/Elite)

### CO-TRUSTEES
If more than one Trustee is serving, all Trustees shall act jointly unless otherwise expressly authorized herein. Any Trustee may delegate ministerial acts to another Trustee, but fiduciary responsibility shall remain joint and several.

### TRUSTEE RESIGNATION
Any Trustee may resign at any time by providing written notice to the Trustor, if living, or to the adult beneficiaries then entitled to receive distributions.

### TRUSTEE REMOVAL
Any acting Trustee may be removed, with or without cause, by the Trustor during the Trustor's lifetime, or thereafter by a majority of the adult beneficiaries.

### ACCOUNTING WAIVER
The Trustee shall not be required to provide periodic accountings to any beneficiary unless requested in writing by a beneficiary legally entitled thereto.

### IN-KIND DISTRIBUTIONS
The Trustee may satisfy any distribution obligation by distributing assets in kind at values determined in good faith by the Trustee.

### RESERVE AUTHORITY
The Trustee may retain reasonable reserves for anticipated expenses, taxes, or liabilities before making distributions.

### BUSINESS CONTINUATION
The Trustee may continue, operate, or liquidate any business interest held in the Trust without liability for loss, provided the Trustee acts in good faith.

### PER STIRPES DISTRIBUTION
If a beneficiary predeceases the Trustor, that beneficiary's share shall pass to his or her descendants by right of representation.

### PER CAPITA DISTRIBUTION
Distributions shall be made per capita to the beneficiaries then living, without regard to lineage.

### 120-HOUR SURVIVORSHIP
A beneficiary must survive the Trustor by at least one hundred twenty (120) hours to be entitled to any distribution under this Trust.

### SIMULTANEOUS DEATH
If the Trustor and a beneficiary die under circumstances making the order of death uncertain, the beneficiary shall be deemed to have predeceased the Trustor.

### AFTER-BORN / ADOPTED CHILDREN
References to "children" include after-born and legally adopted children unless expressly excluded.

### UNEQUAL DISTRIBUTIONS
The Trustee may distribute Trust assets in unequal shares as expressly stated in this Trust without duty to equalize prior lifetime transfers.

### TANGIBLE PERSONAL PROPERTY MEMORANDUM
The Trustee shall distribute tangible personal property in accordance with any written memorandum signed by the Trustor, whether or not incorporated herein.

### MINOR'S TRUST
Any distribution to a minor beneficiary shall be held in trust and used for the minor's health, education, maintenance, and support until the minor attains the age specified herein.

### AGE-STAGGERED DISTRIBUTIONS
Distributions to a beneficiary shall be made in stages upon attaining the ages of ___, ___, and ___ years.

### REAL PROPERTY MANAGEMENT
The Trustee may lease, repair, improve, insure, refinance, or sell any real property held in trust.

### HOA COMPLIANCE
The Trustee shall comply with all homeowners' association rules and may pay assessments and related charges.

### ENVIRONMENTAL DISCLAIMER
The Trustee shall not be responsible for environmental conditions existing prior to acquisition of Trust property.

### PARTITION AUTHORITY
The Trustee may partition or subdivide real property as deemed appropriate.

### DIGITAL ASSETS AUTHORITY
The Trustee is authorized to access, manage, and control digital assets pursuant to applicable law, including the California Revised Uniform Fiduciary Access to Digital Assets Act.

### ONLINE ACCOUNT ACCESS
The Trustee may access electronic communications, cloud storage, and online accounts of the Trustor to the extent permitted by law.

### MEDIATION REQUIREMENT
Any dispute arising under this Trust shall first be submitted to mediation prior to court proceedings.

### ATTORNEY FEES
The prevailing party in any action relating to this Trust may recover reasonable attorney fees from the Trust estate.

### JURISDICTION & VENUE
Any proceeding relating to this Trust shall be brought in the county of the Trustor's residence at death.

## PHASE 3 - ADVANCED CLAUSES (Tier: Elite + Gating)

### DISCLAIMER TRUST AUTHORIZATION
A beneficiary may disclaim all or any portion of a distribution, and any disclaimed interest shall pass as provided herein.

### MARITAL DEDUCTION AUTHORIZATION
The Trustee is authorized to administer this Trust in a manner intended to qualify distributions for the federal estate tax marital deduction.

### QTIP ELECTION AUTHORIZATION
The Trustee is authorized to make a qualified terminable interest property election where permitted by law.

### CREDIT SHELTER TRUST AUTHORIZATION
The Trustee may allocate assets to a separate trust intended to utilize the applicable estate tax exclusion amount.

### GST ALLOCATION
The Trustee is authorized to allocate generation-skipping transfer tax exemption as permitted by law.

### SPECIAL NEEDS TRUST (FRAMEWORK)
Distributions for a beneficiary receiving public benefits shall be made solely in the Trustee's discretion and not for basic support so as to avoid disqualification from such benefits.

### INCENTIVE TRUST
Distributions may be conditioned upon educational attainment, employment, or other milestones as stated herein.

### ASSET PROTECTION TRUST
The Trustee shall administer the Trust to protect assets from beneficiary creditors to the maximum extent permitted by law.

### DECANTING AUTHORIZATION
The Trustee may distribute Trust assets into a new trust with substantially similar terms as permitted by California law.

### TRUST DIVISION
The Trustee may divide this Trust into separate trusts for administrative convenience or beneficiary interests.

### TRUST MERGER
The Trustee may merge this Trust with another trust having substantially similar purposes.

### TRUST PROTECTOR
A Trust Protector may be appointed to modify administrative provisions without altering beneficial interests.

### ARBITRATION CLAUSE
Any dispute arising under this Trust shall be resolved by binding arbitration in accordance with California law.

### CHARITABLE REMAINDER AUTHORIZATION
The Trustee may administer assets as a charitable remainder trust if expressly directed herein.

### CHARITABLE LEAD AUTHORIZATION
The Trustee may create a charitable lead interest prior to distribution to non-charitable beneficiaries.

### PROP 19 AWARENESS CLAUSE
The Trustee shall administer real property transfers with awareness of California property tax reassessment laws but makes no guarantee of tax outcomes.

### FIREARMS TRUST COORDINATION
Any firearms shall be administered in compliance with applicable state and federal law.

### CRYPTOCURRENCY CUSTODY
The Trustee may hold and manage cryptocurrency assets using custodial or non-custodial methods.

### ADVISOR ROLE
The Trustee may rely on a non-fiduciary advisor for investment or administrative guidance.

### NO-CONTEST (IN TERROREM)
Any beneficiary who contests this Trust without probable cause shall forfeit his or her interest to the extent permitted by law.

### BANKRUPTCY PROTECTION
Trust assets shall not be used to satisfy beneficiary bankruptcy claims prior to distribution.

### FORCE MAJEURE
The Trustee shall not be liable for delays caused by events beyond reasonable control.

### INDEPENDENT TRUSTEE SAFEGUARD
Certain discretionary distributions shall require an independent Trustee where required by law.

### CHARITABLE PURPOSE FALLBACK
If a charitable provision fails, assets shall pass to a similar charitable purpose consistent with the Trustor's intent.

### TERMINATION UPON UNECONOMIC ADMINISTRATION
The Trustee may terminate the Trust if its value becomes insufficient to justify continued administration.

### EARLY TERMINATION
The Trust may terminate upon satisfaction of all Trust purposes as determined by the Trustee.

### MERGER WITH POUR-OVER WILL
This Trust is intended to coordinate with any pour-over will executed by the Trustor.

### GOVERNMENT BENEFITS PRESERVATION
No distribution shall be made that would knowingly disqualify a beneficiary from means-tested benefits unless expressly authorized.

### CREDITORS' CLAIMS PROCEDURE
The Trustee may establish reasonable procedures for the resolution of creditor claims.

### INTERNATIONAL ASSETS
The Trustee may hold and manage assets located outside the United States.

### SEPARATE SHARE ACCOUNTING
The Trustee may maintain separate accounting for each beneficiary's share.

### CONTINGENT BENEFICIARY CLASS CLOSING
Beneficiary classes shall close at the time specified herein to permit final distribution.

### FINAL DISTRIBUTION AUTHORITY
The Trustee's determination of final distribution shall be conclusive absent bad faith.

### NON-FIDUCIARY RELIANCE
The Trustee may rely on information provided by others without independent verification unless unreasonable to do so.

### LIABILITY LIMITATION
The Trustee shall not be liable for acts performed in good faith reliance on this Trust.

### SURVIVING SPOUSE ELECTION COORDINATION
The Trustee may coordinate Trust administration with any spousal elections under applicable law.

### RESERVED POWERS CLAUSE
Any powers not expressly limited herein are reserved to the Trustee as permitted by law.
