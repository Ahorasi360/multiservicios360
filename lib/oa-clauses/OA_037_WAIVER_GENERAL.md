## ARTICLE XXXIV â€” WAIVER AND ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

**Section 34.1. No Waiver.** No failure or delay by any Member or the Company in exercising any right, power, or privilege under this Agreement shall operate as a waiver thereof, nor shall any single or partial exercise of any right, power, or privilege hereunder preclude any other or further exercise thereof or the exercise of any other right, power, or privilege. The rights and remedies provided herein are cumulative and not exclusive of any rights or remedies provided by law.

**Section 34.2. Further Assurances.** Each Member shall, at any time and from time to time, execute and deliver such further instruments, documents, and agreements, and take such further actions, as may be reasonably requested by the {{management_reference}} or any other Member to carry out the purposes and intent of this Agreement and to consummate the transactions contemplated hereby.

**Section 34.3. Creditors.** None of the provisions of this Agreement shall be for the benefit of or enforceable by any creditor of the Company or any creditor of any Member, and no creditor who makes a loan to the Company shall have or acquire at any time, as a result of making the loan, any direct or indirect interest in the profits, losses, or property of the Company other than as a creditor.

**Section 34.4. Binding Effect.** This Agreement shall be binding upon and shall inure to the benefit of the Members and their respective heirs, executors, administrators, personal representatives, successors, and permitted assigns. This Agreement shall also be binding upon any Person who acquires a Membership Interest in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.

**Section 34.5. Headings.** The headings and captions contained in this Agreement are for convenience of reference only, shall not be deemed to be a part of this Agreement, and shall not be referred to in connection with the construction or interpretation of this Agreement.

**Section 34.6. Attorneys' Fees.** In the event of any action, suit, or proceeding to enforce, interpret, or construe any provision of this Agreement, the prevailing party shall be entitled to recover from the non-prevailing party its reasonable attorneys' fees, costs, and expenses incurred in connection therewith, in addition to any other relief to which the prevailing party may be entitled.

**Section 34.7. Time of Essence.** Time is of the essence with respect to each and every provision of this Agreement requiring action to be taken within a specified period of time or on a specified date.

**Section 34.8. Pronouns and Number.** Whenever the context requires, the gender of all words used in this Agreement includes the masculine, feminine, and neuter, and the singular number includes the plural, and vice versa.
