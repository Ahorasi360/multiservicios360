## ARTICLE XXIII â€” INSURANCE

**Section 23.1. General Insurance.** The {{management_reference}} shall obtain and maintain, or cause to be obtained and maintained, at the Company's expense, such insurance coverage as the {{management_reference}} deems reasonably necessary or appropriate for the protection of the Company and its assets, including: (a) commercial general liability insurance; (b) property insurance covering the Company's real and personal property; (c) workers' compensation insurance as required by applicable law; and (d) such other insurance as may be required by law or as the {{management_reference}} deems prudent.

**Section 23.2. Errors and Omissions / Professional Liability.** If the Company provides professional services, the {{management_reference}} shall obtain and maintain professional liability insurance (errors and omissions insurance) in amounts and with coverages that the {{management_reference}} deems reasonably necessary given the nature and scope of the Company's professional services.

**Section 23.3. Key Person Insurance.** The {{management_reference}} may, in its discretion, obtain and maintain key person life insurance policies on the lives of one or more Members or key employees of the Company in such amounts as the {{management_reference}} deems appropriate. The Company shall be the owner and beneficiary of any such policies. Each Member agrees to cooperate fully with the Company in obtaining such insurance, including submitting to physical examinations and completing applications. Proceeds from key person insurance shall be the property of the Company and shall be used for the benefit of the Company as determined by the {{management_reference}}.

**Section 23.4. Directors and Officers Insurance.** The {{management_reference}} may, in its discretion, obtain and maintain directors and officers liability insurance (or management liability insurance) covering the Members, Managers, and officers of the Company against claims arising out of their service to the Company, in such amounts and with such coverages as the {{management_reference}} deems reasonably appropriate.

**Section 23.5. Member Cooperation.** Each Member shall cooperate with the Company and its insurance carriers in connection with the procurement and maintenance of insurance coverage, including providing such information and executing such documents as may be reasonably requested. No Member shall take any action that would jeopardize or invalidate any insurance coverage maintained by the Company.
