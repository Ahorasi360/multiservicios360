## ARTICLE II â€” PURPOSE AND POWERS

**Section 2.1. Purpose.** The purpose of the Company is to engage in {{business_purpose_text}}; and to engage in any and all other lawful activities and transactions as may be necessary, incidental, or advisable to carry out the foregoing purpose, including, without limitation, to enter into any partnership, joint venture, or other similar arrangement, to own interests in any other entity, and to do any and all things necessary, convenient, or incidental to the accomplishment of the foregoing purposes.

**Section 2.2. Powers.** The Company shall have all the powers and privileges granted by the Act, by any other law, or by this Agreement, together with any powers incidental thereto, including such powers and privileges as are necessary or convenient to the conduct, promotion, or attainment of the business, purposes, or activities of the Company. Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Company shall not engage in any activity that would require the Company to register as an investment company under the Investment Company Act of 1940, as amended, or that would cause the Company to be classified as an association taxable as a corporation for federal income tax purposes.
