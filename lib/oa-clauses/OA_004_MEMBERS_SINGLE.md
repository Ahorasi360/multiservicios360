## ARTICLE IV — MEMBER

**Section 4.1. Sole Member.** The name, address, Capital Contribution, and Percentage Interest of the sole Member of the Company are set forth in Schedule A attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference. The sole Member shall own one hundred percent (100%) of the Membership Interests in the Company.

**Section 4.2. Limited Liability.** The Member shall not be personally liable for any debt, obligation, or liability of the Company, whether arising in contract, tort, or otherwise, solely by reason of being a Member of the Company. The failure of the Company to observe any formalities or requirements relating to the exercise of its powers or the management of its business or affairs shall not be grounds for imposing personal liability on the Member for liabilities of the Company.

**Section 4.3. Representations and Warranties.** The Member hereby represents and warrants to the Company that: (a) the Member has the legal capacity and authority to enter into this Agreement and to perform the Member's obligations hereunder; (b) this Agreement constitutes the legal, valid, and binding obligation of the Member, enforceable against the Member in accordance with its terms; and (c) the Member's Membership Interest in the Company is being acquired for investment purposes only and not with a view toward the resale or distribution thereof.

**Section 4.4. No Other Members.** As of the date of this Agreement, the Company has no members other than the Member identified in Schedule A. Additional members may be admitted to the Company only in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement and the Act.
