## ARTICLE XIII â€” ADMISSION OF NEW MEMBERS

**Section 13.1. Admission Requirements.** A Person may be admitted as a new Member of the Company only upon the satisfaction of all of the following conditions:

**(a)** The unanimous written consent of all existing Members to such admission;

**(b)** The execution and delivery by such Person of a counterpart of this Agreement or a joinder agreement in form and substance acceptable to the {{management_reference}}, pursuant to which such Person agrees to be bound by all of the terms and provisions of this Agreement as if an original party hereto;

**(c)** The payment by such Person of such Capital Contribution as the existing Members shall unanimously determine;

**(d)** The delivery by such Person to the Company of all information and documentation reasonably requested by the {{management_reference}} for purposes of compliance with applicable laws, including anti-money laundering laws; and

**(e)** Compliance with all applicable federal and state securities laws.

**Section 13.2. Adjustment of Interests.** Upon the admission of a new Member, Schedule A shall be amended to reflect the new Member's name, address, Capital Contribution, and Percentage Interest, and the Percentage Interests of the existing Members shall be adjusted accordingly as agreed upon by the unanimous written consent of all Members (including the new Member).

**Section 13.3. No Third-Party Rights.** Nothing in this Article shall be deemed to create any right in any Person to be admitted as a Member of the Company. The decision to admit a new Member shall be in the sole and absolute discretion of the existing Members.
