## ARTICLE III â€” DEFINITIONS

**Section 3.1. Definitions.** As used in this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth below:

**(a) "Act"** means the California Revised Uniform Limited Liability Company Act, Title 2.6 of the California Corporations Code, Sections 17701.01 et seq., as amended from time to time.

**(b) "Affiliate"** means, with respect to any Person, any other Person that, directly or indirectly through one or more intermediaries, controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with, such Person. For purposes of this definition, "control" (including the terms "controlled by" and "under common control with") means the possession, directly or indirectly, of the power to direct or cause the direction of the management and policies of a Person, whether through the ownership of voting securities, by contract, or otherwise.

**(c) "Agreement"** or **"Operating Agreement"** means this Operating Agreement of {{company_name}}, as originally executed and as amended, modified, supplemented, or restated from time to time in accordance with the terms hereof.

**(d) "Articles of Organization"** means the Articles of Organization of the Company as filed with the California Secretary of State, as amended from time to time.

**(e) "Bankruptcy"** means, with respect to any Person, the occurrence of any of the following: (i) the filing by such Person of a voluntary petition seeking liquidation, reorganization, arrangement, or readjustment of debts under any federal or state bankruptcy, insolvency, or debtor relief law; (ii) the entry of an order for relief against such Person in any involuntary proceeding under any such law which order remains in effect for sixty (60) days; (iii) the appointment of a receiver, trustee, custodian, or similar official for all or substantially all of such Person's property; or (iv) the making by such Person of a general assignment for the benefit of creditors.

**(f) "Capital Account"** means, with respect to each Member, the capital account maintained for such Member in accordance with this Agreement.

**(g) "Capital Contribution"** means, with respect to any Member, the aggregate amount of cash and the initial Gross Asset Value of any property (other than cash) contributed to the Company by such Member.

**(h) "Code"** means the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended from time to time, or any successor statute thereto. Reference to a specific section of the Code shall include such section, any valid regulation promulgated thereunder, and any comparable provision of any future legislation amending, supplementing, or superseding such section.

**(i) "Company"** means {{company_name}}, a California limited liability company.

**(j) "Distributable Cash"** means, as of any relevant date, all cash, revenues, and funds received by the Company from Company operations, less the sum of the following, to the extent paid or set aside by the Company: (i) all principal and interest payments on indebtedness of the Company and all other sums paid or payable to lenders; (ii) all cash expenditures incurred incident to the normal operation of the Company's business; and (iii) such reserves as the {{management_reference}} deems reasonably necessary for the proper operation of the Company's business.

**(k) "Distribution"** means a transfer of money or other property from the Company to a Member on account of such Member's Percentage Interest.

**(l) "Fiscal Year"** means the Company's fiscal year, which shall be the calendar year unless otherwise required by the Code or determined by the {{management_reference}}.

**(m) "Gross Asset Value"** means, with respect to any asset, the asset's adjusted basis for federal income tax purposes, except as otherwise provided in Treasury Regulation Section 1.704-1(b)(2)(iv).

**(n) "Majority in Interest"** means Members holding more than fifty percent (50%) of the total Percentage Interests of all Members entitled to vote on the matter in question.

**(o) "Manager"** means any Person designated as a manager of the Company in accordance with this Agreement, who need not be a Member of the Company.

**(p) "Member"** means any Person who has been admitted as a member of the Company in accordance with this Agreement and the Act and who has not ceased to be a member of the Company.

**(q) "Membership Interest"** means a Member's entire interest in the Company, including such Member's economic interest (right to receive allocations and distributions), governance rights (right to vote and participate in management), and informational rights, in each case as set forth in this Agreement and the Act.

**(r) "Percentage Interest"** means, with respect to each Member, the percentage set forth opposite such Member's name in Schedule A attached hereto, as amended from time to time in accordance with this Agreement.

**(s) "Person"** means any individual, partnership, limited partnership, limited liability company, corporation, trust, estate, association, or any other entity.

**(t) "Tax Matters Member"** or **"Partnership Representative"** means the Member designated pursuant to this Agreement to serve as the partnership representative of the Company under Section 6223 of the Code (or any successor provision).

**(u) "Transfer"** means any sale, assignment, transfer, exchange, gift, bequest, pledge, hypothecation, encumbrance, or other disposition, whether voluntary or involuntary, by operation of law or otherwise.

**Section 3.2. Construction.** Unless the context otherwise requires: (a) the gender (or lack of gender) of all words used in this Agreement includes the masculine, feminine, and neuter; (b) references to Articles and Sections refer to Articles and Sections of this Agreement; (c) references to Schedules are to Schedules attached to this Agreement, each of which is incorporated herein by reference; (d) "including" and "includes" mean "including, without limitation" and "includes, without limitation," respectively; and (e) all references to dollar amounts are to lawful currency of the United States of America.
