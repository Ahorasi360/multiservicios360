## ARTICLE XXVII â€” AMENDMENT

**Section 27.1. Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended, modified, or supplemented only by a written instrument executed by {{amendment_threshold}}. No oral agreement, course of dealing, or course of performance shall be effective to amend, modify, or supplement this Agreement. Any amendment to this Agreement shall be binding upon all Members, their heirs, successors, and assigns.

**Section 27.2. Amendments to Schedule A.** Notwithstanding Section 27.1, Schedule A may be amended by the {{management_reference}} without the consent of the Members to reflect: (a) any Transfer of Membership Interests made in accordance with this Agreement; (b) the admission of new Members in accordance with this Agreement; (c) the withdrawal, resignation, or removal of Members in accordance with this Agreement; and (d) any changes to Members' addresses or contact information. The {{management_reference}} shall provide a copy of any amended Schedule A to all Members promptly following any such amendment.

**Section 27.3. No Impairment Without Consent.** Notwithstanding Section 27.1, no amendment shall: (a) modify the limited liability of any Member; (b) alter the Percentage Interest or economic rights of any Member without such Member's prior written consent; or (c) increase the obligations of any Member to make Capital Contributions without such Member's prior written consent.
