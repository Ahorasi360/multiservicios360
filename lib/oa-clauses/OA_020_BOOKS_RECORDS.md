## ARTICLE XVIII â€” BOOKS AND RECORDS

**Section 18.1. Books and Records.** The {{management_reference}} shall maintain, or cause to be maintained, at the Company's principal office, complete and accurate books and records of the Company, including: (a) a current list of the full name and last known address of each Member; (b) copies of the Articles of Organization, this Agreement, and all amendments thereto; (c) copies of the Company's federal, state, and local tax returns for the three (3) most recent Fiscal Years; (d) the Company's financial statements for the three (3) most recent Fiscal Years; and (e) the Company's books and records as they relate to the Company's internal affairs for the current and past four (4) Fiscal Years. The books and records shall be maintained in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles consistently applied, or such other method of accounting as the {{management_reference}} may determine to be appropriate.

**Section 18.2. Inspection Rights.** Each Member shall have the right, upon reasonable written request and at such Member's own expense, to inspect and copy the books and records of the Company during normal business hours. The {{management_reference}} may impose reasonable restrictions on such inspection rights to protect confidential or proprietary information of the Company. Any inspection must be for a purpose reasonably related to the Member's interest as a Member.

**Section 18.3. Financial Reports.** Within ninety (90) days after the close of each Fiscal Year, the {{management_reference}} shall furnish to each Member: (a) a balance sheet as of the end of such Fiscal Year; (b) a statement of income and expenses for such Fiscal Year; (c) a statement of each Member's Capital Account as of the end of such Fiscal Year; and (d) such other information as shall be necessary for the preparation of each Member's federal, state, and local tax returns.

**Section 18.4. Bank Accounts.** The {{management_reference}} shall open and maintain one or more bank accounts in the name of the Company at one or more financial institutions. All funds of the Company shall be deposited in such accounts, and no other funds shall be commingled therewith. Withdrawals from such accounts shall be made only by Persons authorized by the {{management_reference}}. Company funds shall not be commingled with the personal funds of any Member.
